# Herdr Cheat Sheet

## Launch

- `herdr`
- Help: `Ctrl+A` then `?`
- Settings: `Ctrl+A` then `Shift+S`
- Reload config: `Ctrl+A` then `Shift+R`
- Detach: `Ctrl+A` then `Q`

## Pane

- Focus left: `Ctrl+A` then `H` or `Ctrl+Alt+H`
- Focus down: `Ctrl+A` then `J` or `Ctrl+Alt+J`
- Focus up: `Ctrl+A` then `K` or `Ctrl+Alt+K`
- Focus right: `Ctrl+A` then `L` or `Ctrl+Alt+L`
- Split vertical: `Ctrl+A` then `V` or `Ctrl+Alt+D`
- Split horizontal: `Ctrl+A` then `S` or `Ctrl+Alt+Shift+D`
- Close pane: `Ctrl+A` then `X`
- Zoom pane: `Ctrl+A` then `Z` or `Ctrl+Alt+Z`
- Resize mode: `Ctrl+A` then `R`
- Toggle sidebar: `Ctrl+A` then `B`

## Tab

- New tab: `Ctrl+A` then `T`
- Rename tab: `Ctrl+A` then `,`
- Previous tab: `Ctrl+A` then `P` or `Ctrl+Alt+[`
- Next tab: `Ctrl+A` then `N` or `Ctrl+Alt+]`
- Close tab: `Ctrl+A` then `W`

## Workspace

- Open workspace picker: `Ctrl+A` then `G`
- New workspace: `Ctrl+A` then `Shift+N`
- Rename workspace: `Ctrl+A` then `Shift+W`
- Close workspace: `Ctrl+A` then `Shift+D`

## Notes

- Prefix key: `Ctrl+A`
- Ghostty is the outer terminal; pane/tab/workspace operations live in Herdr.
- Prefix handling is adjusted for macOS IME usage.
